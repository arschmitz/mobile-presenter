LIVE DEMOS === BAD IDEA

<--Notes-->

So raise your hands how many people have seen someone try a live demo at a conference or meetup or whatever and it failed for some reason?

I have seen a lot of them attempted and they are far too often a bad idea only moments in

I bet more then a few of you here have seen a few hiccups at WWDC or similar where apple even though they spend incredible amounts of money testing and preparing still don't have all their demos go off without a hitch. Fate always still seems to way of catching up and throwing a one wrench in the works

But at the same time live demos are exciting an popular and an important part of many talks and presentations
