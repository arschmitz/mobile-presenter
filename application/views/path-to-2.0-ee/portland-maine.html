About Me

<--Notes-->

I'm a programmer and web developer from portland maine

I came to the jQuery Foundation a little over 2 years ago. At the time i had been using jQuery Core and UI for several years in my work, and was starting to use jQuery Mobile for some of my newer projects

At the time i was lucky enough to have been invited to the jQuery Developer Summit where, then project lead Todd Parker approached me about becoming a team member.

From there it was a bit of a whirlwind ride. 2 weeks later i was talking at jQuery Asia in South Korea and i was a full fledged team member with Commit access within a month.

Less then a year after joining the team i took over as Development Lead of the jQuery Mobile when John Bender left the project to go back to School

And most recently in July i became the Project Lead

Professionally I think i am incredibly lucky to be able to now be spending 100% of my development time working with the jQuery Foundation.

Perhaps the best part about working with the jQuery foundation though is the wonderfull group of people i am lucky enough to work with
